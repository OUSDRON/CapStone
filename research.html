The Communal Affects of Gentrification

How does gentrification affect the culture in communities of color?

Throughout the past couple decades, gentrification has become more and more of a threat to historically underfunded, ghetto, and overlooked neighborhoods. But why are wealthier people deciding to move into these communities of lower-class people of color? What does it say about a city's progressive evolution? And how is the displacement of families affecting the culture in these communities?

What are the causes of gentrification?

  1. New business opportunities
  2. Cheaper housing and amenities
  3. City initiative(neighborhood cleanup, potholes fixed, etc)
